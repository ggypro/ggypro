About me :

I'm an Information Technology student from Petra Christian University, Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia. I've spent a lot of time playing games and doing school projects. I started programming in college in 2011, before I chose this major, I chose International business. Instead of going for an International business major, I wanted to know more about games and what goes on behind the scenes, and yeah the first semester was a disaster for me and somehow I regret my decision.

Instead of giving up of what I've encountered, I decided to try my best and I succeeded to overcome it. This major start to change my life, instead of just playing games, I learnt how to make games ,websites, database, and a lot of stuffs that I've never imagined before.

Coming to USA :

Coming to United States was always been my dream and passion! Because the quality of living in US is a lot better compared to Indonesia (imo). There's a lot of tech industries, and technology is a lot better in US. On April 2014, I got a random email (in this case I probably forgot when I apply or subscribe or anything) from MakeGamesWithUs. It's a startup company that help young developers to make games and ship it to the app store! I applied for the Summer Academy program that they made for 2 months.

In short , I got accepted and got a scholarship to come to US and learn how to make games for iOS devices. One day in the office, I looked my self in the bathroom mirror and stared thinking, " I'M NOT IN INDONESIA " , that was an emotional experience for me because, it was a dream come true for me since I've never imagined before that I will come to US in such young age.

What next :

MakeGamesWithUs has helped me learned a lot about programming and "real-life" work scenario. I gave up on my gaming, I left my US$ 1500 brand new PC just for this program. I bought a mac and wanted to focus myself to work harder. I graduated from MakeGamesWithUs on August 16th. Another great thing about US is, even though I'm not that confident with my English, I can improve my English skill just by being here. (you can probably see it by looking at my awesome writing skill).

After seeing a lot of startup in San Francisco which is my place to attend the Summer Academy, I'm interested in founding a startup company. For now a job in US my main target, I'll keep looking for jobs and hopefully awesome things will happen.
